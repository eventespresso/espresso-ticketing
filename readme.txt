Upload the entire ticketing folder to the wp-content/uploads/espresso directory on your server. 

Add the email shortcode [ticket_link] to your custom emails in the Event Espresso > General Settings  page OR when using Email Confirmation area when creating a new event or editing an existing event.

If you have purchased the  printable ticket feature, with custom logo and standard layout. Please email support@eventespresso.com with a copy of your logo in PNG format.
